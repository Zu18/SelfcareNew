💤 Sleep

Getting 7-9 hours of quality sleep is essential for mental and physical well-being. Avoid screens before bed, maintain a consistent sleep schedule, and create a relaxing bedtime routine.

💧 Hydration

Drink at least 2 liters of water daily to stay hydrated. Carry a water bottle, eat water-rich foods, and set reminders to sip water throughout the day.

🧘‍♂️ Meditation

Practice mindfulness for at least 5-10 minutes daily. Focus on your breath, use guided meditations, and find a quiet space to clear your mind.

🏋️‍♂️ Workout

Engage in at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily. Whether it's a walk, yoga, or strength training, staying active boosts mood and energy levels.
